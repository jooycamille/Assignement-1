Purpose

Voice Technology, also known as voice search or voice recognition software are programs that can recognise and record audible commands of the human language and translate them into code i.e. computer language, that can interact with other programs.

Uses:

  • transcription and dictation
  • voice assistans
  • automotive command and control
  • social media messengers
  • customer experience
